‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new subscription?
Tap the "+" button on the main screen, choose a service from the predefined list or create a custom one, enter the details, and save.
Can I customize notifications?
Yes! In each subscription's settings, you can choose how many days before renewal to receive the notification (from 1 to 30 days).
How does data backup work?
Go to Settings > Backup, tap "Export" to save all your data in a JSON file. You can then import this file on other devices.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely! All data is stored only on your device using Apple's SwiftData technology. No information is sent to external servers.
How do I add a widget to the home screen?
Long press on the home screen, tap the "+" in the top left, search for "Tilde" and choose your preferred widget size.
Can I change currency?
Yes, go to Settings > Currency and choose from supported currencies. The change will apply to all new subscriptions.

üõ†Troubleshooting

App won't open or crashes

  • Make sure you have iOS 18.5 or later
  • Restart the app by closing it from multitasking
  • Restart your device
  • Check that you have the latest version of the app

Notifications not working

  • Check that notifications are enabled in iOS Settings
  • Verify app-specific notification settings
  • Make sure the subscription has notifications enabled

Backup issues

  • Verify you have enough space on your device
  • Make sure the backup file is accessible
  • Check that the file is in valid JSON format
